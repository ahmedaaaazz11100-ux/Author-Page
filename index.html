Robert Kiyosaki

About the Author

Robert T. Kiyosaki is an American entrepreneur, investor, and author best known for Rich Dad Poor Dad. He is the founder of the Rich Dad Company, which focuses on financial education through books and seminars.

His work encourages people to build assets, invest wisely, and create passive income in order to achieve long-term financial independence.
